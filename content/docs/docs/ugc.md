## Protocol Overview and UGC Definition

**"User-Generated Content" (UGC)**: Refers to any data and information that you proactively create, upload, publish, or synchronize via the cloud during your use of this Project's services. This includes, but is not limited to:
* Custom coordinates, markers, and route planning created on the map;
* Comments, descriptions, and correction suggestions submitted regarding map coordinates;
* Uploaded third-person perspective screenshots and edit logs;
* Any other interactive content generated by your personal account.

You must assume full responsibility for all UGC generated under your account. You pledge that, when using this Project, it is **strictly prohibited** to create, upload, or disseminate content of the following nature:

1. **Infringing Content**: Content that infringes upon the intellectual property rights, trademark rights, privacy rights, or reputational rights of third parties.
2. **Illegal and Non-compliant Content**: Content that violates the laws and regulations of the People's Republic of China and your local jurisdiction, including but not limited to politically sensitive information, content related to narcotics or gambling, and information propagating violence or terrorism.
3. **Harmful and Offensive Content**: Content containing pornography, insults, defamation, hate speech, discriminatory language, or material severely offensive to others.
4. **Game Violation Content**: Information publishing or promoting cheat tools, illicit means, private servers, or content that disrupts the fairness of *Arknights: Endfield*.
5. **Malicious Disruption and Interference**: Content incorporating malicious code, viruses, spam, or the deliberate uploading of incorrect images or false coordinates designed to mislead other users.
6. **Privacy Theft**: Content that involves the unauthorized collection or publication of others' private data and account information.

## Data Storage and Synchronization Mechanisms

In alignment with this Project's "Local-First" design and [Cookie Policy](../cookie), the storage of your UGC data will vary according to your login status:

* **Logged Out State**: Your UGC data (such as custom markers, preference settings) is stored exclusively in the local storage (`LocalStorage` / `IndexedDB`) of your current device and will never leave your device.
* **Logged In State**: Your UGC data will be transmitted via strongly encrypted channels and synchronized into our **Upstash** (Serverless Redis) and **Cloudflare D1 Database** infrastructure to achieve cross-device consistency. This Project pledges that it will never use your synchronized data for commercial sale or advertising tracking.

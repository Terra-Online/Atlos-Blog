## Who We Are

Open Endfield Map is a community-driven open-source project dedicated to creating the most comprehensive interactive map for the Endfield universe.

## Our Mission

We believe in open data and collaborative development. Our goal is to build a free, accurate, and feature-rich mapping platform accessible to all players.

## History

The project was founded in early 2026 as a response to the growing need for a reliable and up-to-date interactive map for the Endfield game.

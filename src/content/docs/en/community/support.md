## Support the Project

Open Endfield Map is a free, open-source project. Here are ways you can support us:

### Star the Repository

The simplest way to show support is to star our GitHub repository. It helps us gain visibility and attract more contributors.

### Spread the Word

Share the project with friends, forums, and social media. The more people know about it, the better it becomes.

### Report Bugs

If you find a bug, please report it on our GitHub Issues page with as much detail as possible.

### Financial Support

We currently do not accept donations, but this may change in the future. Stay tuned for updates.

## Contact

For support inquiries, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Terra-Online/Atlos).
